INTRESTING NEWS

Now, link PNR nos of connecting journeys online

The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) website and mobile app have introduced a new feature from April 4 where one can link the PNR numbers of two connecting journeys. This is in cases where people have booked tickets between a pair of stations for which direct trains may not be available on that particular date.
This will help passengers to get a refund if they miss their second train because the first train is delayed.

Apple Music prices slashed in India, now cheaper than Spotify

Apple has slashed prices for subscription plans of its music streaming service.Apple Music is now available at a monthly rate of Rs 99 fromRs 120.
and it has also reduced prices of its student, family and yearly plan. Students can now subscribe to Apple Music for a monthly price of Rs 49 from Rs 60 plan. Apple Music monthly family subscription plan is Rs 149 from Rs 190.
The yearly subscription plan is now Rs 999 from Rs 1,200. 

Netflix starts testing Rs 65 mobile-only weekly plan in India

Netflix started testing its cheaper mobile-only subscription plans in India last month. The company has now begun trials of a new mobile-only weekly plan priced at Rs 65 for users in India. Netflix confirmed that it’s new weekly plan in India is only a test and not a price cut.
Users can sign up for the Rs 65 weekly plan along with the 1 month free trial, but note it’s not visible to all users. 

Windows 10: Microsoft improves Game bar with Spotify integration, new features

Microsoft wants you to play more games on your Windows 10 device. The next big update focuses on making gaming more convenient with an improved “Game bar” and more new features integrated. The range of features includes Spotify integration and ability to share in-game content.

Verizon’s first 5G mobile service goes live in two US cities

Verizon Communications Inc., in a tight race with AT&T Inc. and Sprint Corp., has claimed bragging rights as the first U.S. carrier to offer fifth-generation mobile-phone service.
Starting Friday, Verizon customers with unlimited data plans in parts of Minneapolis and Chicago can get speeds of up to 1 gigabit per second on a limited number of phones for an extra $10 a month. The initial launch is part of a 30-city goal for 5G service this year. 

Over 200 million 4G devices sold in India in 2018: Report

Reliance Jio, Xiaomi and Samsung emerged as the top 4G smartphone players with 33%, 20% and 17% market shares, respectively, in 4G smartphones in 2018.
In the 4G tablets category, Lenovo, Samsung and iBall secured 40%, 26% and 14% market shares, respectively.

Snap Games is launching with six games including Bitmoji Party, Alphabear Hustle, Tiny Royale, Snake Squad and Zombie Rescue Squad. Snapchat users will be able to play these games right from the chat bar.

Samsung starts mass production of 5G chips, modem

Samsung Electronics on Thursday said its 5G modem and chips are in mass production for the latest premium mobile devices.
The 5G chipsets include “Exynos Modem 5100” as well as a new single-chip radio frequency transceiver “the Exynos RF 5500”, and supply modulator solution, “the Exynos SM 5800” -- supporting technologies that provide mobile manufacturers with optimum network communication solutions for the 5G era.

IMPORTANT NEWS (4/4/2019)

Madras HC bans TikTok, asks Centre to pass law to protect kids

In an extraordinary move, the Madras high court on Wednesday ordered a ban on mobile music app TikTok, saying addiction to such apps was spoiling the future of the youth and the mindset of children. TikTok, originally known as Musical.ly, is an app created by a Chinese company in 2016. It has 500 million users worldwide, of which over 100 million are in India. In February, Tamil Nadu information technology minister M Manikandan said the state would write to the Centre to push for banning the app.

Jio Acquires Conversational AI Platform Haptik for About Rs. 700 Crores.

Reliance Industries on Wednesday announced that its subsidiary Reliance Jio has acquired Mumbai-based Haptik - one of the world's largest conversational Artificial Intelligence (AI) platforms - for about Rs. 700 crore.Reliance will hold about 87 percent of the business, with the rest being held by Haptik's founders and employees via stock options.

Google Pay leads in UPI transactions by value 

Google Pay has taken the pole position on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) platform in terms of value of transactions by a margin of around 25%. The development comes even as rival Paytm continues to be a leader in terms of transaction volumes, closely followed by PhonePe and Google Pay.

Airtel topped 4G download speeds during Kumbh Mela 2019, claims report.

For the Ardh Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj which happend this year, telcos like Airtel, Vodafone Idea and Reliance Jio had announced special services and special data plans. The Kumbh Mela held between January 14 and March 4, attracted over 100 million people. South Korea first to roll out 5G services, beating U.S. and China

South Korea will become the first country to commercially launch fifth-generation (5G) services on Friday as it rolls out the latest wireless technology with Samsung Electronics' new 5G-enabled smartphone Galaxy S10. With one of the world's top smartphone penetration rates, South Korea is in a race with China, the United States and Japan to market 5G, hoping the technology will spur breakthrough in fields such as smart cities and autonomous cars, and drive up its economic growth that slowed to a six-year low in 2018.

Android Q beta 2 rolls out, brings two major features inspired by Facebook and iOS.

Have you used Facebook Messenger? Did you encounter the Chat Heads that simply pop up with a movable window when someone messages you? Android Q beta 2 now brings this feature to all apps.
It is all about enhancing data privacy and Google is taking a step inspired by Apple's iOS. If you have used an iPhone, you must be familiar with iOS' method of allocating storage to apps - each app gets its own share of storage on the device. With Android Q, Google is doing something similar. 

Foxconn is weeks from trial run to make newest iPhones in India

Foxconn Technology Group is within weeks of starting trial production of the latest iPhones in India as Apple Inc. seeks to revive its fortunes in the country, people familiar with the matter said.
The trial run of the iPhone X range of devices would come before Foxconn starts full-scale assembly at its factory outside the southern city of Chennai, the people said, asking not to be identified as the plans are private. Wistron Corp. already produces older models, such as the iPhone 6s, iPhone SE and iPhone 7, at a plant in Bangalore.
