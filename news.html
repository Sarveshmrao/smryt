IMPORTANT NEWS (3/4/2019)

WhatsApp users can send suspicious messages to the tip line to get verified by PROTO’s verification centre whether the message shared is true, false, misleading, disputed or out of scope.

Ahead of India’s General elections from April 11, WhatsApp has announced the launch of a new tip line to report misinformation and rumors on the platform. The Checkpoint Tip line on WhatsApp +919643000888 has been launched by India-based media skilling startup PROTO.

New WhatsApp privacy: Now users can decide who can add them to groups.

To enable this, users need to go to settings in their app, then tap Account > Privacy > Groups and select one of three options: “Nobody,” “My Contacts,” or “Everyone.” “Nobody” many saw this in the latest beta update of WA Messenger.

“To continue using Facebook, you’ll need to confirm your email,” says the message in the login screen for many, followed by a form asking for users’ email password.

Twitter eases account suspension appeal for users who accounts were suspended.

Twitter will now allow users, (accounts suspended for violating its guidelines), appeal the decision directly within its platform. Earlier, users whose accounts were frozen needed to fill an online form to appeal Twitter’s decision and the response time varied from a few hours to over a week.

TRAI’s deadline for DTH, cable rules extended: Users will be moved to ‘Best Fit plan’.

Telecom Regulatory Authority of India's extended deadline of March 31 for customers to choose their channel has now officially expired and customers will now be moved to the 'Best Fit Plan,' by Direct-To-Home (DTH) providing companies.

Google India's President Rajan Anandan quits and will join venture fund Sequoia Capital.

Google's India and South East Asia President Rajan Anandan has put in his papers after an eight-year stint at the American tech giant. He is also an investor in multiple startups, is slated to join venture fund Sequoia Capital. He will work with Google until the end of this month.

Vikas Agnihotri will take on the responsibility who is currently serving as Google's Country Director for Sales. 

Airtel offering 4G hotspot device for free with six-month rental plans.

Bharti Airtel has revised its hotspot rental plans and is also offering a 4G hotspot device for free after Jio revolutionised the data space in India. Earlier, Airtel had launched the 4G Hotspot at a higher price but the introduction of JioFi by Jio made the telecom operator revise the price of the 4G hotspot and its rental plans. Airtel is now offering 4G Wi-Fi hotspot device at no additional cost when customers pick an advance rental plan of six months.

Amazon's Grocery Store... Amazon Go.

Amazon Go is a chain of convenience stores in the United States operated by the online retailer gaint Amazon, with four locations each in Seattle and Chicago, and two in San Francisco as of April 2019.

Snapchat goes ‘desi’ by introducing four Indian languages.

Snapchat is happy to announce its localization into 4 Indian languages - Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati and Punjabi.

Reinforcing its commitment to the Indian market, Snapchat launched 'Discover in India' last year and is now localizing into regional languages to engage and excite Snap chatters to share content in their very own language," the company said in a press release.

Apple’s Airpower (wireless charging pad) dies before it’s launch.

Apple has cancelled its wireless charging mat, Airpower, that was supposed to power iPhone, Apple Watch and Air Pods all at the same time.
